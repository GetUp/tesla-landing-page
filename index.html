With a rooftop solar + home battery system you can reduce your power bills and your reliance on fossil fuels.

Tesla’s Powerwall 2 is a cutting-edge 14kWh lithium-ion battery pack that can store solar energy from your rooftop for whenever you need it - day or night.

With double the capacity of the original, the Powerwall 2 contains a liquid thermal control system, an integrated inverter and intelligent software to ensure you’re making the most of your solar system.

High Capacity

With 13.5kwh of storage, 91.8% round trip efficiency and 100% depth of discharge, Powerwall 2 has capacity to spare for evening use or back-up power when the grid goes down

All-in-One

Powerwall 2's integrated inverter reduces costs, streamlines installation, and makes it easy to connect to any solar system

Weather Smart

Water resistant and equipped with a liquid thermal control system, Powerwall 2 can be installed indoors or outdoors and operate in temperatures from -20°C to 50°C

Cutting-Edge

Users can monitor usage and storage data from their system in real-time using the My Tesla web site and mobile app, available for Android and Apple iOS devices
